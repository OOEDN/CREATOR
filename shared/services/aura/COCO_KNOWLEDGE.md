## Brand Identity

### What is OOEDN?
OOEDN (Out Of Eden) is a premium streetwear and lifestyle brand. The aesthetic is bold, minimalist, and unapologetically premium. Think: the intersection of high fashion and street culture.

### Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Bold, confident, minimal. Never try-hard.
- **Visual**: Dark backgrounds, emerald accents, sharp typography, high contrast
- **Copy Style**: Short. Punchy. Lowercase when casual. ALL CAPS for emphasis. No exclamation marks unless it's fire.
- **Do**: Be direct, be cool, be premium
- **Don't**: Be wordy, be generic, use buzzwords, over-explain

### Products
- **Scalp Milk**: Signature product line. Premium positioning.
- Brand categories span lifestyle, beauty, and streetwear
- Pricing reflects premium quality — never discount unnecessarily

## Creator Operations

### Creator Pipeline
1. **Discovery** → Find creators via Brillo, Social Cat, Join Bands, or organic scouting
2. **Outreach** → First contact via email or DM. Use OOEDN voice.
3. **Negotiation** → Rates vary by platform, reach, and engagement. Track in creator profile.
4. **Onboarding** → Ship product, provide brief, set expectations
5. **Content Creation** → Creator produces content per brief
6. **Review & Approval** → Quality check against brand standards
7. **Publishing** → Scheduled posting with proper disclosures
8. **Follow-up** → Track performance, maintain relationship

### Creator Statuses
| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Active | Currently working with us |
| Long-Term | Established, recurring partnership |
| Inactive | Paused — may reactivate |
| Blackburn | Do not contact. Serious issues. |

### Red Flags
- Creator asks for payment before shipping product
- Significantly lower quality than portfolio samples
- Late delivery without communication (>7 days past deadline)
- Posting without proper #ad/#sponsored disclosure
- Reusing content from other brand deals

### Outreach Templates (Adapt Per Creator)
**Initial Reach**:
> "{name}, we've been watching your content and love what you're doing with [specific content reference]. We think you'd be a perfect fit for OOEDN. Interested in a collab?"

**Follow-Up (No Response)**:
> "Hey {name} — just floating this back up. We'd love to send you some product and see what you create. No pressure — just think you'd vibe with the brand."

**Post-Delivery Check-In**:
> "Hey {name}, hope the package landed safely! Take your time with the content — just wanted to make sure everything arrived in good shape."

## Content Strategy

### Hook Frameworks (0-3 seconds)
1. **Negative Assumption**: "Stop doing [X] if you want..."
2. **Curiosity Gap**: "I tried [Product] so you don't have to."
3. **Visual Pattern Interrupt**: Sudden zoom, color shift, unexpected movement
4. **Hyper-Specificity**: Target a visceral feeling ("that feeling when...")
5. **Controversial Take**: Bold opinion that stops the scroll

### Content Quality Checklist
- [ ] Hook grabs within first 3 seconds
- [ ] Brand/product visible within first 5 seconds
- [ ] Audio is clean (no background noise)
- [ ] Lighting is intentional (not dark/blown out)
- [ ] #ad or #sponsored visible in first 3 seconds of overlay
- [ ] CTA is clear (soft or hard)
- [ ] Aspect ratio matches platform requirement

### Platform Requirements
| Platform | Aspect Ratio | Max Length | Best Practices |
|----------|-------------|------------|----------------|
| TikTok | 9:16 | 3 min | Hook in 1s, trending audio, raw/authentic feel |
| Instagram Reels | 9:16 | 90s | Polished aesthetic, strong visuals, save-worthy |
| Instagram Stories | 9:16 | 15s/slide | Casual, behind-the-scenes, swipe-up CTA |
| YouTube Shorts | 9:16 | 60s | Value-packed, educational angle works well |

## Operational Workflows

### Shipment Flow
1. Creator approved → Create shipment record (status: Preparing)
2. Package shipped → Add tracking number, update status to Shipped
3. AI tracking syncs status automatically
4. Delivered → Status updates, follow-up timer starts (7 days)
5. If no content after 14 days → Coco suggests nudge email

### Payment Protocol
- Payment ONLY after content is delivered and approved
- Track payment method per creator (Venmo, PayPal, Zelle)
- Mark payment status: Unpaid → Pending → Paid
- Keep rate history for negotiation reference

### Email Operations
- All outreach from: create@ooedn.com
- Bulk emails use {name} personalization
- AI Polish before sending (tone + grammar)
- Track thread history for context

## Compliance & Legal

### FTC Requirements
- #ad or #sponsored must appear in the first 3 seconds
- Disclosure must be clear and conspicuous (not buried in hashtags)
- If AI-generated content is used, label it appropriately
- Keep records of all creator agreements and disclosures

### Content Rights
- Ensure usage rights are agreed upon before publishing
- Creator retains original content; OOEDN gets licensing rights per agreement
- Never repost without creator credit unless explicitly agreed
